Choose Your Faction

UEF

United Earth Federation - Industrial might and heavy armor. Reliable, versatile units with powerful shields and artillery.

Cybran

Cybran Nation - Stealth, speed, and unconventional warfare. Excels at harassment, cloaking, and hit-and-run tactics.

Aeon

Aeon Illuminate - Precision and range. Hover units, powerful beam weapons, and the strongest experimental units.

Seraphim

The Seraphim - Alien technology with regeneration and unique abilities. Powerful but requires mastery to use effectively.
