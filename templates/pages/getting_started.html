Getting Started

New to FAForever? This guide will help you get up and running in no time.

1

Get the Game

You need a copy of Supreme Commander: Forged Alliance. Get it on Steam or GOG for about $15.

Steam
2

Download FAF Client

Download and install the FAForever client. It handles patching, matchmaking, and mods automatically.

Download
3

Create Account

Create your FAF account. Use the same username you want to play with - it's permanent!

Register
4

Join Discord

Join our Discord for help, community chat, and finding games. The #help channel is great for new players.

Discord
